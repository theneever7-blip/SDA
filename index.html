Key Beliefs

The Sabbath

We observe the seventh-day Sabbath as a day of rest and worship, following God's example in creation.

The Second Coming

Jesus Christ will return to earth soon, and we live in anticipation of that glorious event.

Health Message

Caring for our bodies as temples of the Holy Spirit through healthy living and lifestyle choices.

Bible Authority

The Bible is the inspired Word of God and the foundation of all our teachings.
