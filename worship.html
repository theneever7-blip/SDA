Worship & Lifestyle

Experience the joy of Sabbath worship and discover a lifestyle that honors God in body, mind, and spirit.

Sabbath Worship

What to Expect in a Sabbath Service

  • Song Service: Congregational singing of hymns and worship songs
  • Prayer: Opening and closing prayers, and special prayer requests
  • Sabbath School: Bible study in small groups
  • Sermon: Message from God's Word
  • Communion: Foot washing and Lord's Supper (quarterly)

Sabbath services typically begin at 9:30 AM and conclude around 12:00 PM, followed by fellowship time.

Adventist Lifestyle

Nutrition & Health

We believe in caring for our bodies as temples of the Holy Spirit. Many Adventists follow a vegetarian diet and avoid harmful substances.

Education

Adventist education emphasizes academic excellence, spiritual development, and service to others.

Community Service

Serving others is at the heart of our faith. We run hospitals, schools, and humanitarian programs worldwide.

Family Life

Strong families are the foundation of society. We promote healthy relationships and moral living.
